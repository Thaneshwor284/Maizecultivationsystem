Maize Cultivation Guide

Climate Requirements

Maize thrives in temperatures between 20-30°C with moderate humidity.

Water Management

Regular irrigation needed, especially during the growing season.

Soil Preparation

Well-drained, fertile soil with pH between 5.5-7.0 is ideal.

Growing Season

Best planted during spring in Kathmandu region.

Fertilizer Suggestions

Current Season: Winter

Recommended Fertilizer: Urea and Potash for root development.

Common Diseases & Remedies

Maize Streak Virus

Remedy: Use resistant varieties and control vector insects.

Leaf Blight

Remedy: Apply fungicides and practice crop rotation.
